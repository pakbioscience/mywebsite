Our Mission

We aim to match the most outstanding students in the biosciences from Pakistan to centers of excellence in academia and industry in the US based on a binational collegial network of scientists. In this way, we strengthen global cooperation in the health sciences and make a unique contribution to the development of future leaders for the Pakistan health-science community.

To achieve this aim, we strive to

  • Organize workshops to identify scientific talent
  • Form focus groups in cross-disciplinary areas that underpin modern research to promote collaborations
  • Offer guidelines for application to graduate and internship programs in the U.S
  • Offer online seminars and discussion groups to hone problem-solving skills

Pakistan Bioscientist Association is going to be registered as a non-profit organization
